# User Roles & Permissions

The platform is built on trust. As you contribute more, you earn more trust and capabilities.

## 1. Student (The Beginner)
Every new user starts here.

*   **View**: can read all wiki pages for their university.
*   **Propose**: can *suggest* edits to pages. These edits go into a "Pending" queue.
*   **Vote**: can upvote/downvote content.
*   **Profile**: can customize their bio and social links.

## 2. Trusted User (The Regular)
Students who have made a significant number of approved contributions.

*   **Auto-Approve (Limited)**: can make up to 5 edits per day that go *live immediately* without moderation.
*   **Badge**: displays a "Trusted" badge on their profile.
*   **Mentor**: can vouch for new students (future feature).

## 3. Staff (The Moderator)
University staff or highly trusted student leaders.

*   **Unlimited Auto-Approve**: all their edits go live immediately.
*   **Moderate**: can approve or reject "Pending" edits from Students.
*   **Manage**: can create new Institutes and Labs.

## 4. Admin (The Operator)
The system operators.

*   **God Mode**: full access to everything.
*   **University Management**: can add new universities and email domains.
*   **User Management**: can ban users, promote to Staff, or reset passwords.
*   **System Settings**: control global notifications and backups.
